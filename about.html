About

My Story

Hello! Im a Spanish-Italian, born and raised in Madrid. Started my nomadic life at 19, always been passionate about motorbikes, mechanics, outdoors and art, among others. Started customizing motorbikes and stencil spray painting since an early age.

During my trips I discovered the underwater world of scuba diving, since then I felt in love with light, shadows and underwater landscapes, becoming a technical diver and underwater photographer specialised in shipwrecks and chiaroscuro photography. Im just starting a new path in the tech industry as a Designer.
