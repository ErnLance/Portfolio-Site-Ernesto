Burgering

Burgering is a web responsive app where users can find all kind of burger recipes, designed to offer a futuristic street vendor looking mixed with an actual luxury restaurant style. In this app, users are able to save the recipes and change ingredients, depending on what they have available at home. As a burger recipe app, it needed to have a large menu and at the same time be easy to navigate and fit all formats from desktop and tablets to smartphones. The objective was to create a burger recipe site without loosing the quality of a good recipe app. Fitting all devices and being user friendly, giving the user not only the option to navigate easy but also having all that they need in one app.
