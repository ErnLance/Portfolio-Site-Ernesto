Burgering

Burgering is a web responsive app where users can find all kind of burger recipes. In this app, users are able to save the recipes and change ingredients, depending on what they have available at home. The objective was to create a burger recipe site without loosing the quality of a good recipe app.

My process

Ideation

Inspired by futuristic street vendors and luxury gourmet restaurants, mixed with the more traditional burger & bbq style.

Wireframes

As a burger recipe app, it needed to have a large menu and at the same time be easy to navigate and fit all formats from desktop and tablets to smartphones.

Design

Choosing colours and typography that would give futuristic feelings, beeing easy to read and creating a neon style looking.

Prototype

The rersult is userfriendly app, fitting all devices, giving the user not only the option to navigate easy but also having all that they need in one app.
