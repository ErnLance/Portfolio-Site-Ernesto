Ictum

Ictum, from the Latin: Beat, Pulse, Tempo, or the entry of part of a section of the orchestra. Ictum is a music player app with a vintage looking developed to fit armoniously more modern colours. The idea was to create a music player app without unnecessary getaways, the rersult is a userfriendly app, with all the necessary features.
